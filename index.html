hackMHS II
Millburn, NJ. May 21-22, 2016.

a hackathon focused
on community

hackMHS is about building a close community in which students of all backgrounds can make new friends, learn new skills, and grow as developers.


Whether you love music, gaming, design, hardware, biology, or more, come to hackMHS to discover where your interests meet technology, and collaborate with other enthusiastic students to build a cool project.

frequently asked questions

  • Who can attend?

    Any high school student is welcome to apply! If you're in middle school, you may attend if you are at least 13 years old. If you're in college and would like to help, let us know—there are plenty of ways you can get involved!

  • What if I've never been to a hackathon before?

    You can let us be your first :) In fact, hackMHS is designed with first-time hackers in mind, with numerous mentors and workshops to help guide you along the way.

  • How much does it cost?

    Nothing at all! hackMHS is completely free to all participants. In fact, while you're here, we'll load you with plenty of food, snacks, and awesome swag.

  • How long will the event last?

    hackMHS will start Saturday morning and end Sunday afternoon. The hacking period will be 24 hours; projects started before the hacking period will not be eligible for submission.

  • When will applications open?

    Applications will open March 24th, and close April 24th!

  • What should I bring?

    Bring your laptop, phone, chargers, and excitement about learning new things!

  • How will you decide between applicants?

    Participants will be selected by a random lottery. The first round of accepted students will be notified by mid-May and will have 5 days to RSVP before their spots are released to students on the waitlist.

  • Do I need to join a team beforehand?

    Nope! In fact, we highly encourage you to meet new students during our team formation activities at the beginning of the event. You are welcome to form a team of up to 4 students.

  • Will you provide travel reimbursement?

    We're currently unable to provide travel reimbursement, but this is subject to change in the coming months.

  • How do I get there?

    hackMHS takes place at Millburn High School (462 Millburn Avenue, in Millburn, New Jersey). For Google Maps directions, click here.

  • How can I get involved?

    If you are a college student or industry professional, you can come as a mentor or present a workshop/talk—contact us if you are interested! If you are a MHS student, you can join our organizing team next year.

  • I have a question that isn't answered here.

    Feel free to email us your questions at hackatmhs@gmail.com, or message us via our Facebook page. We'll be happy to answer them!
